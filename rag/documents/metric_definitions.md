# InsightPilot AI Metric Definitions

## Total Revenue

Total revenue is the sum of revenue from qualifying completed orders.

Cancelled orders must not be included in total revenue.

## Gross Revenue

Gross revenue is the total value of qualifying orders before subtracting refunds.

## Net Revenue

Net revenue is calculated as:

Net Revenue = Gross Revenue - Approved Refund Amount

## Average Order Value

Average order value is calculated as:

Average Order Value = Total Qualifying Revenue / Number of Qualifying Orders

Only qualifying completed orders should be included in this calculation.

## Total Orders

Total orders represent the number of qualifying orders placed during the selected reporting period.

## Active Customers

An active customer is a customer who has placed at least one qualifying order within the defined activity period.

## Churn Rate

Churn rate is calculated as:

Churn Rate = Number of Churned Customers / Total Customers

The result may be displayed as a percentage.

## Refund Rate

Refund rate is calculated as:

Refund Rate = Number of Refunded Orders / Number of Qualifying Orders

## Customer Lifetime Value

Estimated customer lifetime value represents the expected revenue contribution of a customer based on purchase history, average order value, purchase frequency, and customer lifespan.

## Purchase Frequency

Purchase frequency is calculated as:

Purchase Frequency = Total Orders / Customer Lifetime Duration

## Days Since Last Order

Days since last order is the number of days between the current reference date and the customer's most recent qualifying order date.
