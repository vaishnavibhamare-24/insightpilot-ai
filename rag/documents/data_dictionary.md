# InsightPilot AI Data Dictionary

## Customers

### customer_id

Unique identifier for a customer.

The customer identifier must not be null.

### total_orders

Total number of orders associated with the customer.

### lifetime_revenue

Total qualifying revenue generated by the customer.

### average_order_value

Average qualifying revenue generated per qualifying order.

### first_order_date

Date of the customer's first qualifying order.

### last_order_date

Date of the customer's most recent qualifying order.

### days_since_last_order

Number of days between the reference date and the customer's most recent qualifying order.

### customer_lifetime_days

Number of days between the customer's first order and the current reference date.

### purchase_frequency

Measure of how frequently a customer places orders.

### estimated_clv

Estimated customer lifetime value.

### churn_label

Binary customer churn indicator.

Allowed values:

- 0 means active.
- 1 means churned.

## Orders

### order_id

Unique identifier for an order.

### customer_id

Identifier of the customer associated with the order.

### order_amount

Monetary value of the order.

### order_status

Current business status of the order.

## Refunds

### refund_amount

Monetary value of a refund.

An approved refund should not exceed its associated order amount.

## Revenue Features

### monthly_orders

Number of qualifying orders during a month.

### monthly_customers

Number of customers with qualifying activity during a month.

### gross_revenue

Total qualifying revenue generated during a month.

### units_sold

Total number of product units sold during a month.
