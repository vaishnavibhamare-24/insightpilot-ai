# InsightPilot AI Dashboard Guide

## Purpose

The InsightPilot AI dashboard provides business users with visibility into customer, revenue, churn, refund, and data quality metrics.

The dashboard supports business monitoring and data-driven decision making.

## Revenue Dashboard

The revenue dashboard may display:

- Gross revenue.
- Net revenue.
- Monthly revenue trends.
- Average order value.
- Total orders.
- Monthly customer count.
- Units sold.

Revenue metrics should use validated qualifying order data.

## Customer Dashboard

The customer dashboard may display:

- Total customers.
- Active customers.
- Churned customers.
- Churn rate.
- Customer lifetime value.
- Purchase frequency.
- Customer segments.

## Churn Dashboard

The churn dashboard may display:

- Customer churn probability.
- Churn risk category.
- Low-risk customers.
- Medium-risk customers.
- High-risk customers.

High-risk customers have a churn probability of 0.70 or greater.

## Refund Dashboard

The refund dashboard may display:

- Total refunds.
- Approved refund amount.
- Refund rate.
- Refund data quality issues.

Refunds exceeding the original order amount should be flagged for review.

## Data Quality Dashboard

The data quality dashboard may display:

- Total rows evaluated.
- Missing critical values.
- Invalid churn labels.
- Failed validation checks.
- Data quality alert status.

## AI Guidance

InsightPilot AI may use dashboard definitions to explain business metrics and reporting views.

If dashboard information is not documented in the enterprise knowledge base, the system should state that the information is not available.
